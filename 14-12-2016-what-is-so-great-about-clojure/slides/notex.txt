Het is een Lisp.
A Lisp is a language that is written in an AST (not syntax), when the language is interpreted or compiled these code trees can be manipulated.
Anderen zeggen verlichting. Ik hoop jullie daar wat van te laten zien.
It is quite common, and easy, for Clojure programs to manipulate, transform and produce other Clojure programs.
